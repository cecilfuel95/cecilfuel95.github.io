Biography

Jill Ellen Stein (born May 14, 1950) is an American physician, activist, and politician.

She is currently the Green Party's nominee for President of the United States in the 2016 election. Stein was the presidential nominee of the Green Party in 2012, in which she received 469,501 votes (0.4%). She has also twice been a candidate for Governor of Massachusetts -- in 2002 and 2010.
