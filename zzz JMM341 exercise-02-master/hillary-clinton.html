Biography

Hillary Diane Rodham Clinton (born October 26, 1947) is an American politician and the nominee of the Democratic Party for President of the United States in the 2016 election.

She served as the 67th United States Secretary of State from 2009 to 2013, the junior United States Senator representing New York from 2001 to 2009, First Lady of the United States during the presidency of Bill Clinton from 1993 to 2001, and First Lady of Arkansas during his governorship from 1979 to 1981 and from 1983 to 1992.
